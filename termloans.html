Long Term Loan

12.0% P.A

Interest Rate

Loan Amount: Rs. 12,00,000/- Maximum

Re-payment Period: 120 EMI/ Installment

Installment: Rs. 17,217/-

Loan Eligibility:

  • Members should have completed One year of service OR should have completed one year membership in society.
  • Long term loan may be granted to any eligible member 30 times of his gross salary or Rs 1200000.00( Rupees Tweleve Lakh Only) whichever is less by the Board.
  • The loan shall be granted on personal security with two sureties who are members of the society and having more than 01 year of regular continuous service in the establishment.
  • Repaying capacity shall be considered by taking into account net salary though the Bye Law of the society provides loan facility on the basis of gross salary.
  • Explanation: Repaying capacity of loanee be considered as an amount derived after deducting at least 1/3 of the gross salary from the net salary receivable by the loanee member.
  • At no point of time home take salary contribution be less than 1/3 of total emolument/gross salary.


Documents for Long Term Loan :

  • Duly filled Application form of loan.
  • Applicant's latest salary slip of previous two months with signature
  • Latest salary slip of two sureties with signature (member can be surety to maximum two loanee member)
  • Two signed cheques of applicant's salary a/c
  • Photocopies of Aadhaar card & PAN card
